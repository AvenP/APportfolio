About Me

In ten years working in youth sports I have always had a passion to learn the computer side of the field. I was given the opportunity to learn how to run season building and player registration applications. After some time I became the go-to person to complete seasons for multiple locations.

In my leisure I have built custom databases for a few PC games. They were meticulous processes that took weeks, sometimes months, to complete. The largest database I have made was a thirteen-tier football pyramid in Football Manager.

With my work history and my interest in programming I have decided to make a career change.

I am excited to learn and grow in the field!
